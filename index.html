About EcoFluxion

From Data to Intelligence, From Research to Product.

EcoFluxion is an Ankara-based AI R&D company operating in ODTÜ Teknokent. We build our own products in natural language processing (NLP) and large language models (LLM), with a strong focus on Turkish-language AI. Our flagship product is İçtiHub, an AI-powered legal-tech platform.

Our mission is to build AI products that bring large language models and data analytics into real-world use — starting with the legal domain through İçtiHub — and to create lasting value for the people and institutions who rely on them.

Our vision is to become one of Türkiye's leading AI product companies, developing our own platforms that make a difference both locally and internationally.

Our Products

The AI products we build and operate

Our Flagship Product

İçtiHub

Our flagship AI-powered legal platform: integrated archives, an advanced case-law search engine, and intelligent legal-assistance tools running on secure cloud infrastructure. Built for lawyers, legal departments, and institutions across Türkiye.

AI-Powered Archive Smart Template System AI-Powered Case Law Search Document Automation Legal AI Assistant
AI Engine

MevzuatBot

Advanced large language model (LLM) specifically designed for Turkish legal texts, integrating multiple AI components including RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), MCP server agent system, and semantic understanding capabilities. These powerful components power our İçtiHub platform.

Large Language Model Turkish NLP AI Model Training Legal AI Agent System RAG Technology

A large-scale platform we built and operate for industrial green transformation — bringing together environmental data, resources, and a knowledge-sharing ecosystem for academia, industry, and the public sector.
